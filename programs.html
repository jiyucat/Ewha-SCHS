Music Therapy Internship

Gain hands-on experience by working directly with individuals with disabilities at SCHS. This program is designed for advanced students and professionals in music therapy.

Who can join: Graduate students, professionals

Professional Training Program

Enhance your skills through expert-led training sessions focused on advanced techniques in music therapy. The program is tailored for SCHS staff and other professionals in the field.

Who can join: SCHS staff, experienced therapists

Community-Based Music Therapy

Participate in community workshops that use music therapy to improve social interaction, emotional well-being, and collective creativity.

Who can join: General public, community leaders
